Kerbal Simpit Controller

A custom Arduino Mega flight panel for Kerbal Space Program with staging, telemetry displays, joystick attitude control, throttle encoder, action groups, and status LEDs.

Controls

Joystick

Normal mode controls pitch and yaw. Clicking the joystick toggles roll mode, where the X axis controls roll only.

Stage System

Stage button starts a launch countdown before first stage, then performs instant staging. LCD encoder click resets the stage digit to blank.

Telemetry

16x2 I2C LCD pages show ascent, orbit, maneuver, and target data from KerbalSimpit.

Displays

One 1-digit display shows stage countdown/state. One 4-digit display shows USSR standby, wait, or altitude.

Build Notes

The controller uses KerbalSimpit, LiquidCrystal_I2C, Encoder, and SevSeg. Buttons and toggles are wired to ground and use Arduino internal pullups.

The 4-digit display is directly multiplexed by the Arduino. A driver module such as MAX7219 or TM1637 would make the display steadier under heavier serial and LCD traffic.
